> [!Note]
> Shift from the mindset of attention as a limited resource, and create a virtuous cycle where your personal life feeds the professional life and viceversa

The classical [[Ego Depletion|ego depletion]] view is limited. Don't treat what you learn at work as separate from what you learn in your personal life.
Three tips:
1) **to manage your energy, manage your focus**. You can expand your energy by directing the focus on activities that feed your curiosity
2) **reflect on how your energy flows between various areas of your life**. Try to create synergies between different areas of your life
3) **surround yourself with energy expanders**. Connect with the right people
