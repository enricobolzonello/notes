- reality is full of details you don't see immediately
- important details are basically invisible, but when you notice them they become integral part of your intuitive model of the world --> something like [[Hindsight Bias]]
- easy to get stuck in your way of thinking due to invisible details and transparent (obvious) details
- **how to improve**: 
	- seek detail you would not normally notice about the world
	- when you hear arguments that feel wrong, try to notice what details seem important to them and why
