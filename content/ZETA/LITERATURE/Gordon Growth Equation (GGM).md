> Helps in determining a stock's intrinsic value based on expected future dividends growing at a constant rate

$$
P = \frac{D_1}{r-g}
$$
where:
- $P$, current stock price
- $D_1$, value of next year dividends
- $g$, expected growth rate
- $r$, constant cost of equity capital
