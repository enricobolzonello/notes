[How to Do Great Work](https://paulgraham.com/greatwork.html#f17n)
- be curious
- consistency -> don't get a lot done on one day, get small things done each day
- use undirected thinking -> subconscious works even when we are not approaching the problem directly
- aim to be the best to be good
- don't try to work in a distinctive style, just try to do the best job
- *earnest*
- be original in choosing the problem, not only on the solution
- good questions are crucial
- be prolific
